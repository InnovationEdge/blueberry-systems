What we design

Every engagement starts with discovery — stakeholder interviews, user research, competitive audit. We then move into IA, wireframes, and high-fidelity Figma design with weekly reviews and a clean handoff to engineering (your team or ours).

  • User research & discovery Interviews · usability tests · competitive audit · jobs-to-be-done
  • Wireframing & prototyping Information architecture · low-fi flows · interactive Figma prototypes
  • High-fidelity UI design Figma · pixel-precise · responsive · light + dark themes
  • Design systems Tokens · component libraries · Figma variants · Storybook
  • Mobile app design iOS HIG · Material Design · native gesture patterns · haptics
  • Brand identity & visual design Wordmarks · logo systems · color · typography · motion language

Design principles

We design for clarity over decoration, accessibility over aesthetics (then both), and conversion over impressions. Every screen ships with explicit acceptance criteria — what should the user do, what happens if they don't, and what the success metric is.

Accessibility from day one

WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and focus, reduced-motion support, semantic HTML in handoff specs, and keyboard-first interaction design. Built in from the wireframe stage, not retrofitted at the end.
